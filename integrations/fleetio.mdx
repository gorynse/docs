If you run your fleet in **Fleetio**, Rynse connects to it so your vehicles and wash activity stay in sync — no double entry.

<Note>
  When your organization is connected, a blue **Synced with Fleetio** banner appears above your vehicle list. Fleetio is your system of record — make vehicle changes in Fleetio, not by emailing support, and they flow into Rynse automatically.
</Note>

## What you get

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Vehicle sync" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Your Fleetio vehicles flow into Rynse automatically. Add, edit, or retire a vehicle in Fleetio and Rynse stays current — you manage your fleet in one place.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Wash history in Fleetio" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    Every completed wash is pushed back to Fleetio as service history, so each vehicle's record shows when and where it was washed.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Total cost of ownership" icon="chart-line">
    Because wash costs land in Fleetio alongside fuel and maintenance, your cost-of-ownership per vehicle includes washing automatically.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
