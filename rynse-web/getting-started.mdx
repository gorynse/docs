Rynse Web is the management console for your fleet. This page walks you through your first session: signing in, picking the right organization, and orienting to the left-hand navigation.

## Sign in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the dashboard">
    Go to [app.gorynse.com](https://app.gorynse.com). The sign-in page is shared across all customers and partners.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Sign in with the email your fleet admin invited. First time? Check your inbox for an invitation email and follow the password-set link before signing in here.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your organization">
    If you belong to more than one organization, an **organization selector** appears at the top of the left sidebar. Use it to switch between them — every screen in Rynse Web is scoped to the currently selected org.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Forgot your password? Use the **Reset password** link on the sign-in page. The reset email comes from `no-reply@gorynse.com` — check spam if it doesn't appear within a minute.
</Tip>

## The left sidebar

Rynse Web's navigation is grouped to match the way fleets actually run. Here's what each section is for:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Reports" icon="chart-mixed">
    Where you look first thing in the morning.

    - **Dashboard** — at-a-glance metrics, transactions broken out by category (Car Wash, Fuel / Gas, EV, Preventative Maintenance), and recent activity.
    - **Fleet Wash Health** — your composite health score for wash program engagement.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Organization" icon="building">
    The records that everything else hangs off of.

    - **Facilities** — locations where your vehicles live.
    - **Vehicles** — your fleet roster, card assignments, and active memberships.
    - **Users** — the people on your team with access to this org.
    - **Car Wash Locations** — a map of accepting car wash vendors.
    - **Purchase Orders** — for organizations that route spend through POs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Mobile Wash" icon="spray-can-sparkles">
    - **Work Orders** — pending and completed on-site mobile wash jobs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Other" icon="ellipsis">
    - **Visits** — every wash visit across your fleet, with vehicle, location, and timestamp.
    - **Incidents** — flagged issues raised against transactions or visits.
    - **Invoices** — billing history and any open balances.
    - **Settings** — org preferences and integrations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Roles and what you'll see

What you see in Rynse Web depends on your role. The most common roles:

| Role | Sees |
| --- | --- |
| **Fleet manager** | The full Reports + Organization sections, including vehicle management and dashboard reports |
| **User** | Limited views — typically just Users and Invoices for their own org |
| **Vendor** | The vendor dashboard (work orders, history) instead of the fleet manager dashboard |
| **Rynse admin** | An additional **Rynse** section for cross-org administration |

<Note>
  If a menu item described above isn't visible to you, that role doesn't have access. Ask your fleet manager (or the Rynse team) if you think you should.
</Note>
